About Coastal Vista

We are a professional drone photography and videography service based along the Texas Gulf Coast. Our passion is capturing breathtaking aerial imagery that tells your story from a perspective only the sky can offer.

From commercial real estate and construction progress documentation to special events and creative projects, we bring FAA-certified expertise and artistic vision to every flight.

Our Services

Professional aerial imaging for every need

Aerial Photography

High-resolution aerial photos for real estate, construction, events, and creative projects. Delivered in full resolution with professional editing.

Aerial Videography

Cinematic 4K aerial video with smooth, stabilized footage. Perfect for promotional content, documentaries, and social media.

Real Estate

Showcase properties from above with stunning aerial views. Highlight lot size, surrounding areas, and unique features buyers want to see.

Document your build from start to finish with scheduled aerial surveys. Ideal for project managers, investors, and stakeholders.
